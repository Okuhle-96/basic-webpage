Historical Places

District 6 Meseum

  • The District 6 Meseum is an old Methodist Mission Church, it was established in 1867.
  • People of all colours - residents, immigrants, artisans, coloureds - owned and rented houses here, they lived in harmony.
  • In 1901 all the black people were forced to move out and as the decades passed it became a coloured community.

    • City Hall

  • This building was designed as a result of a public competition.
  • The City Hall's carillon was installed as a World War 1 memorial, with 22 additional bells being added in 1925 with the visit of the Prince of the Wales..

    • Church Square

  • Church Square is one of three earliest areas of property in Cape Town development and was defined in 1979.
